❓ How to Play

Welcome to GrandStake! This is a live multiplayer horse racing game where you bet real money via GCash. Here's everything you need to know to get started.
🚪 Joining a Room
1

Enter Your Nickname

Type a unique nickname in the lobby. This is how other players will see you during the race.

2

Choose a Room

Select an available room from the list (green dot = open). Rooms with a red dot are already racing. Tap Join Selected Room to enter.

3

Create Your Own Room

Tap Create New Room to become the host. You choose the number of horses (2–10). As host, you control when the race starts.

🐎 Placing a Bet
1

Pick Your Horse

In the Pick Horse panel, tap on a horse to claim it. Each horse has a unique color and number. Only one player per horse!

2

Set Your Bet Amount

Use the preset buttons (₱50, ₱100, ₱200, ₱500, ₱1K) or type a custom amount. Minimum bet is ₱1.

3

Pay via GCash

Tap Pay via GCash & Lock Bet. A screen will appear with the host's GCash number and the exact amount to send.

💳 GCash Payment Process
1

Send the Exact Amount

Open your GCash app and send the exact peso amount to the number shown. Double-check the account name before sending.

2

Enter Your Reference Number

After sending, GCash will give you a 13-digit reference number. Enter this in the Reference Number field to prove your payment.

3

Wait for Host Confirmation

The host will verify your payment. Once confirmed, your status shows ✓ Ready. The host starts the race when all players are ready.

🏆 How the Winner is Determined
1

Seeded Race — Fully Fair

Each race uses a cryptographically random seed set by the host. All players see the exact same race — there is no manipulation possible.

2

First Horse to the Finish Line

The horse that crosses the finish line first wins. Horses have random bursts of speed, energy curves, and rubber-band mechanics to keep races exciting.

3

Payout

The winner receives 90% of the total pot. The remaining 10% is a service fee retained by the host. If no player bet on the winning horse, the host issues refunds.

💡 Tips: Watch the live positions panel on the right during the race to track your horse. Use the zoom buttons to get a closer look at the action. Good luck! 🍀
